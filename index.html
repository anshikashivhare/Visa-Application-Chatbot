Overview

This guide helps you prepare and submit a visa application. Use the chatbot (bottom-right) to ask quick questions.

Eligibility

  • Check the visa category requirements for your purpose (tourism, work, study).
  • Nationality, travel history, financial sufficiency and health checks may apply.

Required Documents

  1. Valid passport (min 6 months validity)
  2. Passport photos meeting specifications
  3. Completed application form
  4. Proof of funds / bank statements
  5. Invitation letter / acceptance / job offer (if applicable)
  6. Travel itinerary and accommodation

Step-by-step Application

  1. Gather documents listed above.
  2. Fill out the official online form or paper form.
  3. Book appointment (if required) and pay fees.
  4. Attend biometrics/interview (if required).
  5. Wait for decision and collect passport.
